About Us

Jontro-Tantrik Lab is a cutting-edge R&D organization and technical training center founded in September 2016. We specialize in embedded systems, Internet of Things (IoT), robotics, machine learning, and biomedical engineering. Our lab has been at the forefront of innovation in smart healthcare, industrial automation, and agricultural technology.


Led by Md. Anowar Hossain, an Electrical and Electronics Engineer with over 10 years of experience in embedded R&D, mechatronics, and IoT, our multidisciplinary team bridges the gap between theoretical research and practical applications. We actively contribute to the global scientific community through publications in leading journals and conferences.


Our mission is to empower students, researchers, and industries with hands-on training, innovative solutions, and collaborative research opportunities in emerging technologies.

üíì IoT-Based Health Monitoring System

Overview

A comprehensive wearable health monitoring system that continuously tracks vital signs including heart rate, SpO2, body temperature, and sends data to cloud for remote monitoring by healthcare professionals.

Key Features

  • MAX30102 pulse oximeter for heart rate and SpO2 measurement
  • MLX90614 non-contact temperature sensor
  • ESP32 for WiFi connectivity and data transmission
  • Real-time alerts via GSM module for critical conditions
  • Cloud dashboard for doctors and caregivers
  • Low-power design with battery backup

Technologies Used

ESP32, MAX30102, MLX90614, GSM Module, ThingSpeak/Firebase, Arduino IDE, C/C++
